Ever since the pandemic began people struggle to resume a normal life.

The pandemic might of had us quarentined for a few months, but we still have to continue to maintain ourselves.

Many of us lost our jobs and have to rely on government assitance for a substantial living.

However, with lower Contamination and everyday testing facilities in the city of New York; we can now breath a little more open space. The city of New York has been going through phases to open for the public and people can enjoy outdoor activities and dining. All of this is possible if we continue to use everyday protection and hygienic care. But with all these attractions open there are also hard working people making this happened. It is a fortunate but risky thing to have a job in times like this but, there are jobs available for those who need it. It might not be everyone's situation but, for those who have families to attend or have bills to pay like rent their is hope.

Also with more testing site open to the public free of charge, its easier to stay safe and connected with love ones or whenever your traveling.

It's always good to know your status even if you weren't expose to anyone who might have had symptoms or were traveling.
